## ✨ About

This project is a small interactive gift I created for my boyfriend's birthday.

Instead of making a traditional card, I wanted to build something he could explore at his own pace—a collection of memories, little surprises, handwritten notes, and tiny interactive details that remind me of him.

Every photo, sticker, animation, and message was chosen with him in mind.

---

## 📖 What's inside?

- 📸 Childhood photos
- 🤍 Our favorite moments together
- 📝 Things I love about him
- 💌 A birthday letter
- 🎁 A surprise ending
- ✨ Hidden clickable stickers
- 🎵 Background music
